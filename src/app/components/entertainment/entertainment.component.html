Entertainment

Most people visit Taniti to enjoy its stunning beaches, explore the lush rainforest, and marvel at the active volcano. However, Taniti offers a diverse range of activities to suit every interest. These include:

  • Exploring the local history at the Museum of Tanitian History
  • Embarking on chartered fishing tours
  • Experiencing the underwater world through snorkeling adventures
  • Zip-lining through the verdant rainforest canopy
  • Relaxing in various pubs, including a local microbrewery
  • Dancing the night away at a trendy dance club
  • Catching a movie for a laid-back evening
  • Taking to the skies with helicopter rides for breathtaking views
  • Enjoying family fun at the local arcade
  • Exploring art galleries for a touch of culture
  • Engaging in a friendly game of bowling

Additionally, golf enthusiasts can look forward to the opening of a nine-hole golf course next year. Many of these activities are centered around Merriton Landing, a rapidly developing area on the north side of Yellow Leaf Bay.
